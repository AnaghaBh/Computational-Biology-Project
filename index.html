Model

State variables

The model tracks two fields as functions of position z (mm) and time t (s). The coordinate z is in the co-moving frame, so z = 0 always marks the peak of the bacterial band. Negative z is behind the band (depleted food), positive z is ahead (fresh food).

The perceived signal

Bacteria respond to a logarithmic transformation of the aspartate concentration.

ΦA = ln(1 + A / Ki)

Numerical implementation

dt < dz² / (2D)

The PDEs are solved using explicit Euler time-stepping with central finite differences on a 300-point grid, with no-flux boundary conditions.
